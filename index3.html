About us

Owned and operated by the Toomey family since 1974, Tejas Toyota is actively involved with community projects in its hometown of Humble, Texas, and in the neighboring communities of Kingwood, Atascocita, Humble, Huffman, Porter, Spring, New Caney and all of Northeast Houston. Our Internet Department has a dedicated staff to assist you with all of your online needs. Tejas Toyota's factory certified sales staff consist of Master Certified Sales Consultants with membership in Toyota's Sales Society. Onsite Toyota Certified Service Consultants are backed with factory trained Toyota Certified Master Diagnostic Technician's and ASC Certified Technician's
