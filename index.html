Genral information

Toyota's history begins in the 19th century, when Sakichi Toyota invented the first electric saddle machine in Japan, which revolutionized the country's textile industry. In January 1918, Sakichi set up a Toyota sawmill and knitting company, and with the help of his son, in 1924, he realized his lifelong dream of building an automatic sawmill. 2 years later he created "Toyoda Automatic Loom Works".
